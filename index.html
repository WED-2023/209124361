About Me

My name is Graham Foster. I was born in 1944 in a small town near Queenstown, New Zealand. I spent most of my life raising sheep on the hills and playing jazz guitar in the evenings. I met my wife Margaret in 1970, and together we raised three wonderful children. Now retired, I enjoy gardening, storytelling, and playing music with my grandchildren.

One of the people who influenced me most was my high school music teacher, Mr. Wilkins, who encouraged me to explore jazz. His passion shaped the soundtrack of my life.
